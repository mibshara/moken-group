Entreprenuership Academy is a hands on program which gives aspiring entrepreneurs the tools and real life experience necessary to not only start a business but to operate a business in a manner that will enable the maximization of returns. We believe an entrepreneurs success is built on a foundation on of strategy, execution, and dedication.

Our team of mentors and program leaders are experienced entrepreneurs who have a wealth of knowledge that can assist in creating a clear vision of business goals. Each topic offered has a robust curriculum designed to prepare an entrepreneur to successfully launch and operate your business.

  • 01.
    hands on startup experience

    Unlike that of any other entrepreneurship program we’ve seen, entreprenuers are placed on one of five startup teams and handed the reigns of a tech startup to build, develop, and run for the duration of the program.

    Entreprenuers will be intellectually challenged, through working, competing, and collaborating with some of the brightest, most interesting people we’ve ever me. Just like with our hiring practices, we strive to make startup teams as diverse as possible. Providing the opportunity to work with entrepreneurs of all backgrounds, from students to biologists to astronauts, we practive diversity at every turn.

  • 02.
    extensive course curriculum

    Entrepreneurship Academy’s curriculum is extremely comprehensive, offering a vast array of standard business topics paired with numerous specialty workshops ranging from investment pitching to business model and monetization mastery.

    Entrepreneurs will have the opportunity to learn in a variety of settings including; large group seminars, small group workshops, team focused environments, as well as one-on-one sessions designed specifically for an entrepreneur on almost any topic.

  • 03.
    mentorship

    Entrepreneurs will gain access to numerous industry experts and mentors in a diverse array of professions for independant learning opportunities. Intended to support the personal and professional growth of our entrepreneurs, we provide access in a variety of forms.

    Additionally, entreprenuers who are selected at the end of the program to stay on as partners and founding members of their startups will recieve unlimited access to resources and benefits provided through Moken Group’s MokenLabs program for early stage startups, including access to mentorship and a global network of fellow founders.

  • 04.

    Our mission is to use education as a tool to champion positive change in the modern workplace. Change which improves the human experience whilst also providing tangible business benefits.

    Not only will entrepreneurs and startup teams receive access to a comprehensive resource package, mentorship, and added benefits, all entrepreneurs and startup teams will be invited and encouraged to attend all Elevate by Moken events, courses, and workshops (some worth up to $1200) — free of charge.

academy curriculum
week one
ideation & innovation

While getting acquinted we explore the ins & outs of ideation & innovation through a series of team activities & workshops. In addition, product teams are assigned & be founders handed the reigns of five brand new tech startups to lead for the duration of the program.

week two
product perfection

We will explore product/market fit where it is crucial to success. But first, we will identify differentiating factors of an MVP while harnessing the power of necessary features for your market, are what often makes or breaks a tech product & startup within a live market.

week three
model mastery

Besides a good product, business models are what can take a company from being a decent idea to a unicorn. We will analyze what makes a business model sound and how to turn it into something that drives a product to become a revolutionary global success.

week four
converting customers

Through learning to generate higher conversions throughout the entire CLM (Conversion Marketing Lifecycle) we will dive deep into exploring the ways to convert more viewers into visitors, visitors into leads, leads into buyers and buyers into die hard fans.

week five
marketing & growth

From the ins and outs of most social channels, Google Analytics & ads to organically targetted PR & media, we will explore the skills to expertly handle marketing & growth while not forgetting about high-level strategy to create success-driven campaigns.

week six
KPIs & cohesion

KPI & team cohesion are disciplines that involves articulating what an startup is trying to accomplish & identifying the most meaningful & useful indicators of success. We will learn how to build, deploy, & sustain KPIs, while improving strategic or operational performance.

week seven
managing finances

The management of finances is crucial to a successful business; financial information provides the evidence to support decisions in all levels of business, and often helps to justify those decisions when presented to other key stakeholders.

week eight
pitch
perfect

We will dive deep this week into awareness & effectiveness of selling. Learning how to perfectly pitch an idea, company, & self through the marriage of storytelling, fact giving, & inspiration, leading to presenting real investment pitches to raise real capital.

week nine
culture & leadership

Believe it or not, culture and leadership is your number one competitive advantage. But, this is only true if your team understand this. We explore why culture keeps employees engaged and how to use culture to drives revenues, growth, & product development.

week ten
pivot + launch

Finally, this is where it all comes together. Each product team will host a product launch, marketing launch campaign, and have the opportunity to pitch to a panel of investors, & compete for their last chance at a full-time founding member of their tech team.
