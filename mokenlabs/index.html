the program
become a part of the future.

We’re looking for disruptors that are avid learners to collaborate and create with. We want innovators that are looking at market verticals relevant to us with a fresh perspective and the passion necessary to build something that will transform industries.

We are looking for founders, ideas, and startups active in the industry verticals of fashion, technology, energy, pharmaceuticals, healthcare, fitness, transportation, and finance. Our selection process includes a completed application form, due diligence on your business idea, leadership team and competitive differentiation in the market, and interview with the Moken Group partners.

apply today learn more
the application
phase one
initial application

Startups submit their initial application. This form briefly allows us to get acquainted with who you are and what your idea or startup is. Think of this as a simple screening phase, so remember, be sure to put your best foot forward as first impressions are important!

phase two
company & market fit

After review, founders will then be invited to complete an interactive form to dive deeper into the startup, the market in which it operates, and additional relevant venture information to help the MokenLabs team make an educated decision.

phase three
meet & greet interview

In the final phase of the selection our Moken Partners will get to know you even better through an interview conducted remotely to best understand your passion, conduct due diligence, & learn how MokenLabs can best provide resources effectively.

phase four
terms & your offer

You can breathe easy, you’re in the clear & have been tentatively accepted into the program. Any remaining due diligence happens here, along with being presented a custom program offer outlining what MokenLabs can provide for your unique situation.

phase five
onboarding & setup

Welcome! Upon acceptance of terms, you are now a successful member of the MokenLabs community. We will then onboard you, getting you all set up within the necessary schedules, events, and legal documentation to take you to the next level.
