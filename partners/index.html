referral partners
build connections, make money.

We have developed a referral program to show our appreciation to our clients, contacts, and partners who assist with our continued growth by sharing with others the unique problems Moken solves. We offer a partnership bonus to anyone who connects us with an individual or venture we end up doing business with.

This partnership opportunity is as straightforward as can be. For any contact you connect us with, we will offer you 10% of profits. All that we require you to do is to introduce us via email, over the phone, or even in person to a key business contact that you think fits with Moken. We will do all of the heavy lifting and hard work. Simply introduce us, and we take it from there while you sit back and watch the money roll in.
